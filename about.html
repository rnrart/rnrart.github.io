About

Reza N. Roshandel started to create his artworks over 25 years ago. He found that art was the best way for him to express his unutterable feelings and experiences. He has worked in different areas, including conceptual, decorative, ornamental, and functional art, as well as architecture, interior, and sculpture. Reza's main focus in various of his works is to give old materials a new life. He uses recycled materials not only to bring to light the impact of the waste but also to provide context and make useable artworks out of unusable stuff.


Reza believes in creativity and innovation. He builds unique artworks, custom modern sculptural handmade furniture and interior items in various materials and sizes. Feel free to contact for new design ideas, orders, and collaboration!
